Functional IT Designs

Are you looking to build a system, product or service from scratch or to overhaul an existing one? Our functional design (FD) team will focus on the actions and flows to ensure you optimize your process flow and maximize your earned value. Our functional design specification (FDS) identifies what its design object is to do and is more concerned with what is to be done and less with how it happens. Design and development projects go through several phases, which are typically requirements analysis, functional design, detail design, unit and system testing, documentation, and implementation. Our functional design process provides a translation between the requirements analysis and the detail design; running through technical designs and prototyping
